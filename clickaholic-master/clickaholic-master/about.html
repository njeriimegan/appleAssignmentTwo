About Us

Welcome to our travel blog, we love to travel the globe, we have been to so many beautiful places and met interesting people the world, this website is more like our journal.
We are a group of friends who have a passion to travel and explore what our beautiful world has to offer starting with our beautiful country,Kenya.

We decided to document our travels so as to make it easier for you to plan and to explore in terms of budgets, where to get food and supplies and also locations,something we struggled with in the beginning of our travel adventures

We also of course have a selfish motive:to relive the beauty this world has to offer.
So if you love to travel and also have a burning love for nature you are in the right place,let us enjoy this beautiful world together.
